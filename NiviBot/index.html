AICTE

AICTE is a government body that had authority of planning and coordinating the development of technical education throughout India. AICTE faces a number of queries from the user-end be it the institutions, universities or various other organisations. Thus, to reduce the manual work for the client as well as the government, the automation of the queries calls for this AI powered chatbot. This initiative has been taken for approaching the Frequently Asked Questions in a better and efficient manner.
